Half of Aussie adults expected to buy ticket for Powerball’s $100m prize

The numbers for the third-biggest lottery in Australian history has just been drawn.

AU News: One lucky Aussie could be holding the winning lotto ticket for the $100m jackpot, as the numbers have now been revealed.

The massive prize was up for grabs on Thursday, was drawn at 9pm with only a single ticket claiming all the numbers.

The winning numbers are: 16, 24, 11, 4, 10, 8, 23 and the Powerball number is 6.

The Lott spokeswoman Anna Hobdell said up to half of all Aussie adults were expected to grab a ticket in the hopes they’ll win the top prize.
